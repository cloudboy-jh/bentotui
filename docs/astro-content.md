## Positioning

- BentoTUI is not a framework you fight.
- BentoTUI is not a low-level primitives tutorial.
- BentoTUI is the fastest path to shipping terminal apps in Go.

## Core pillars

### Bricks

Official components with strong defaults. Copy and own the source with
`bento add`. Tweak variants and behavior to match your product.

### Rooms

Named layout patterns for full pages: app shells, sidebars, dashboards,
and diff workspaces. Pick a room per page, then place your bricks.

### Bentos

Complete templates that prove real app composition. Start from a bento,
swap data and interactions, and ship.

## One-day promise

You should be able to pull `registry/bentos/home-screen`, wire your data,
add pages with `rooms.*`, and have a serious app shell in one day.

## Messaging guardrails

- Talk in Bento terms: bricks, rooms, bentos.
- Treat Charm/Bubbles as implementation details.
- Show outcomes and ready-to-use app patterns.
- Avoid framework-heavy language.
